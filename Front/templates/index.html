Welcome to Blingbling
Blingbling is an ultra-hip light up necklace you will see all the coolest kids wearing at the next rave.

Blingbling in action

About

Blingbling was made at PennApps 2014. Blingbling was inspired by a group of passionate hackers, seeking to bring coolness to every party. It is our core belief that no party should have to suffer from a lack of flashy synchronized lighting. We are currently looking for rockstar developers that share our vision to join the team.

Blingbling processes audio data and visualizes sounds via LED leights. Blingbling incorporates a variety of high technology components, including the Lilypad audrino, USB because we couldn't figure out how to use bluetooth, and proprietary sound processing algorithms used to process frequency and timing of music.
